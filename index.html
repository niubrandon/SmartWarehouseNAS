Smart Warehouse
Why Smart Warehouse?

Applying blockchain technology for warehouse application to improve the transparency, reliability and efficiency of inventory transactions.

  • All inventory transactions are recorded in the public blockchain.
  • Zero downtime associated with server failure or corruption
  • High transaction speeds
What does Smart Warehouse do?
  • Manage inventory by adding (inbound) or removing (outbound) inventory to/from your physical storage.
  • Search for all transactions associated with a specific part number and show the remaining inventory balance (eg, P0001).

This application can be extended to a full Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) tool for warehouse management by integrating other functionalities such as keeping track of inventory costs, occupancy analysis and warehouse performance.
